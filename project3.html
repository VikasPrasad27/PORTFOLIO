    PROJECT OVERVIEW

    EngiLearn is a comprehensive web platform designed to streamline and enhance the learning experience for engineering students. It offers branch-specific study materials such as previous year papers, reference books, YouTube tutorials, and career roadmapsâ€”all in one place, making it easier for students to access relevant resources.

    The platform incorporates real-time collaboration through Socket.io, allowing students to post doubts and receive peer support instantly. It also features an AI-powered chatbot that provides personalized assistance, helping with doubt resolution, study tips, and career guidance. Additionally, EngiLearn offers a dedicated space for sharing and discovering hackathons and competitions, encouraging students to apply their knowledge practically.
